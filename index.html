About FHAM 2024

The FHAM is the only culinary event in this part of the world which is supported by 2 National Chefs Associations – The Chefs Guild of Lanka and The Chefs Guild of Maldives and Endorsed by the WorldChefs. FHAM offers an unrivaled opportunity for interested trade organizations of the Hospitality Industry to showcase their latest products, services and innovations. An all-inclusive platform for whose objectives are to create an interactive forum for exhibitors and clients. FHAM Serving the Hospitality Industry of Maldives

Dhivehi Expo Services is an organization established by the pioneers of the Exhibition, Conference and Special Event Industry in Maldives with the ambition of developing an organization that could cater to the ever-changing and dynamic demand for such events in Maldives.

Dhivehi Expo Services works hand in hand together with CDC Events & Travels, a regional organizer with a global perspective. Backed by a large network of business professionals representing a diversified sector in Sri Lanka and the South Asian Region along with an extensive operation carried out in Seychelles, Dubai, and India as well.

    “Chefs Competing for Honours”, FHAM 2024 The International Culinary Challenge supported and endorsed by the World Association of Chefs Societies (WACS) is widely recognized as the region’s most prestigious International Culinary Competition that has a strong emphasis in promoting culinary excellence.

    This annual competition is the key decisive platform on which hundreds of Culinarians converge to display their skills, and seek accreditation and feedback for improvement from an Internationally Acclaimed Panel of Judges.
