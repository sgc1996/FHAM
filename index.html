ABOUT FHAM 2024

FHAM (Food and Hospitality Asia Maldives) Global Culinary Challenge and Exhibition is an annual event of great significance held in the beautiful island nation of the Maldives. It is considered the most significant gathering of its kind in South Asia and is widely recognized as the Premier International Hospitality Trade Fair. The 2023 edition of FHAM took place from the 8th to the 10th of May, continuing its tradition of showcasing the finest in the world of food, hospitality, and culinary arts.

The FHAM event serves as a vital platform for various stakeholders within the food and hospitality industry. These stakeholders include international and local businesses, culinary experts, resort operators, and hospitality enthusiasts, among others. The event's multifaceted nature contributes significantly to the Maldives' thriving hospitality sector and the broader South Asian region.

CULINARY CHALLENGE

    “Chefs Competing for Honours”, FHAM 2024 The International Culinary Challenge supported and endorsed by the World Association of Chefs Societies (WACS) is widely recognized as the region’s most prestigious International Culinary Competition that has a strong emphasis in promoting culinary excellence.

    This annual competition is the key decisive platform on which hundreds of Culinarians converge to display their skills, and seek accreditation and feedback for improvement from an Internationally Acclaimed Panel of Judges. When Chefs Compete You Win! This year too, as in the past we are expecting an exuberant number of entries

MOCKTAIL COMPETITION

    “Bartending is a form of artistic expression, extending beyond merely concocting a beverage. It involves the harmonious combination of flavors, techniques, and creativity to craft an exceptional drink. In light of this, the 'FHAM Mocktail Competition 2024' is scheduled to be held in the Maldives, with the goal of identifying and rewarding outstanding bartenders.

    This competition has become an integral part of the contemporary hospitality industry and is globally renowned. Such competitions have the potential to significantly influence the standards of the local food and beverage sector, contributing to its elevation on the international stage.

BARISTA COMPETITION

    Welcome to the FHAM Barista Championship 2024, where the artistry of being a barista takes center stage. Beyond the simple act of crafting a beverage, being a barista is where creativity flourishes a seamless fusion of flavors, aromas, techniques, and innovation to present an extraordinary cup of coffee.

    In line with this philosophy, the 'FHAM Barista Championship 2024' is set to grace the shores of the Maldives, with the primary objective of identifying and rewarding the crème de la crème of baristas. This competition has become an integral part of the coffee industry, resonating globally as a highly anticipated event.

HOUSEKEEPING COMPETITION

    This is an inter-resort/hotel competition. Participation is limited to 1 team of 2 per resorts/hotels/guest houses each team will be provided with 1 “queen size bed” with bedding and linen. Maximum 60 minutes to be given for preparation + decoration time. This helps to give the participants a tangible use for the bed decorations and designing skills.

    The Challenge encourages teamwork, innovation and passion among all the attendants. Working in a small group can educate and help the average performing housekeeping individuals to come up to the hotel standard. In addition to convincing them, the importance of it and how it’s connected to the guest’s satisfaction.
