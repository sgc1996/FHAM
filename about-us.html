WHAT IS FHAM

FHAM (Food and Hospitality Asia Maldives) Global Culinary Challenge and Exhibition is an annual event of great significance held in the beautiful island nation of the Maldives. It is considered the most significant gathering of its kind in South Asia and is widely recognized as the Premier International Hospitality Trade Fair. The 2023 edition of FHAM took place from the 8th to the 10th of May, continuing its tradition of showcasing the finest in the world of food, hospitality, and culinary arts.

The FHAM event serves as a vital platform for various stakeholders within the food and hospitality industry. These stakeholders include international and local businesses, culinary experts, resort operators, and hospitality enthusiasts, among others. The event's multifaceted nature contributes significantly to the Maldives' thriving hospitality sector and the broader South Asian region.

One of the noteworthy aspects of FHAM is its role in driving innovation and promoting international collaboration in the industry. The 2023 edition saw an impressive 150 international product launches, showcasing the latest innovations in food and hospitality. These launches are a testament to the event's ability to attract leading industry players who choose FHAM as the stage to introduce their cutting-edge products and services.

In terms of attendance, FHAM 2023 was nothing short of a spectacle. It drew a staggering 4,700 visitors, further solidifying its reputation as a must-attend event for anyone connected to the food and hospitality sector. The presence of 120 exhibitors representing over 10,000 international brands highlighted the global appeal of FHAM. This diverse exhibitor base offered attendees a comprehensive view of the latest trends and developments in the industry.

The participation of 120 resorts in FHAM 2023 is a testament to the event's appeal to the tourism sector, a crucial component of the Maldivian economy. These resorts, known for their exquisite accommodations and world-class cuisine, actively engage in the event, enhancing their culinary offerings and showcasing the unique experiences they offer to their guests. The direct participation of resorts further emphasizes the event's practical relevance and its role in driving improvements in the local hospitality sector.

The benefits of FHAM are not limited to the event's immediate stakeholders; they extend to the broader economy and the local community. The event boosts tourism by attracting visitors from around the world and supporting local businesses, such as hotels, restaurants, and transportation services. Additionally, it creates opportunities for local talent in the hospitality and culinary sectors, fostering skill development and employment prospects.

The impact of FHAM goes beyond the economic sphere. It also serves as a hub for knowledge sharing and skill enhancement. The culinary challenges and exhibitions allow chefs and culinary enthusiasts to learn from each other, exchange ideas, and grow in their craft. Furthermore, the event provides a space for thought leaders and experts to discuss emerging trends and challenges in the industry, contributing to its continuous evolution.
