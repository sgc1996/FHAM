FHAM EXHIBITION

FHAM stands as a premier platform for trade organizations within the Hospitality Industry to present their newest products, services, and innovations. It serves as an all-inclusive forum aimed at fostering interaction between exhibitors and clients. By providing this platform, FHAM not only promotes economic diversification but also stimulates growth in service sectors.

Exhibitors at FHAM have the unique opportunity to showcase their latest offerings to a diverse audience of industry professionals and decision-makers. This exposure enables them to gain visibility, attract potential clients, and expand their market reach. Moreover, FHAM facilitates networking and collaboration, allowing exhibitors to forge valuable partnerships and explore new business opportunities.

In addition to its role in driving economic growth, FHAM also plays a vital role in promoting innovation within the hospitality industry. Exhibitors are encouraged to showcase their most innovative products and services, thereby contributing to the overall advancement of the industry. This focus on innovation helps to raise standards of quality and service, ensuring that the hospitality sector remains competitive and dynamic.

Furthermore, FHAM serves as a platform for knowledge exchange and professional development within the industry. Through seminars, workshops, and networking events, participants can gain insights into emerging trends, best practices, and cutting-edge technologies. This exchange of ideas and experiences helps to enhance the skills and capabilities of industry professionals, ensuring that they remain at the forefront of their field.

FHAM SERVING THE HOSPITALITY INDUSTRY OF MALDIVES

Dhivehi Expo Services is the driving force behind FHAM, an organization established by pioneers in the industry with a vision to meet the ever-changing and dynamic demands for events in Maldives. With over 22 years of experience, They have a colossal operational portfolio in organizing exhibitions for various sectors such as the Hospitality Industry, Marine, Boating and Fisheries Industry, Construction Industry, Education, and Youth Development in Maldives.

Dhivehi Expo Services is backed by a vast network of business professionals representing diverse sectors in Sri Lanka and the South Asian Region. Their extensive operations are not limited to Maldives but also extend to Seychelles, Dubai, and India. Among the staging of succesull events, Dhivehi Expo Services have made FHAM the gateway to exploring novel trends and developments essential for theever-growing economy of Maldives. Their experienced directors are committed to providing the stakeholders with a unique platform to discover,connect, and thrive in the thriving business landscape of Maldives.
