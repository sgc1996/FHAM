HOUSEKEEPING COMPETITION

Home Housekeeping Bed Decorating Competition

WHAT IS FHAM INTERNATIONAL HOUSEKEEPING COMPETITION ?

This is an inter-resort/hotel competition. Participation is limited to 1 team of 2 per resorts/hotels/guest houses each team will be provided with 1 “queen size bed” with bedding and linen. Maximum 60 minutes to be given for preparation + decoration time. This helps to give the participants a tangible use for the bed decorations and designing skills.

The Challenge encourages teamwork, innovation and passion among all the attendants. Working in a small group can educate and help the average performing housekeeping individuals to come up to the hotel standard. In addition to convincing them, the importance of it and how it’s connected to the guest’s satisfaction.

LEARNING EXPECTATIONS

  1. Know the different types of Beds.
  2. Understand why it’s important to the Guest and Management.
  3. Make the right choices when it comes at the very last minute.
  4. Understand how important it is to design the beds according to the occasions of the customers.
  5. Make the decorations with different materials.

OBJECTIVE - After completing this competition participants will be able to make bed decorations up to the hotel standards and will be following uniformity among all the attendants. Also be able to act on last minute requests and changes.
